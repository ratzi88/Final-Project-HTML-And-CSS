Snowy Cookies

                A recipe for addictive snowy chocolate cookies!
				Cookies with cakey fudge texture and moist inside with a texture reminiscent of brownies.

Cool Fact

A little history about these cookies: Chocolate crinkle cookies were invented by Helen Fredell in St. Paul, Minnesota, in the early half of the 20th Century. They were sold in our school canteen and they instantly became my favourite snack.

Prerequisites

  • 💡Expereince Level: Beginner
  • ⏲Preparation: 20 minutes
  • ⏲Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • ⏲Total Time: 30 minutes
  • 👥Serving Size 4 People

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cups sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • sugar powder

Instructions

  1. Mix the eggs, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the cocoa powder and combine.
  3. Add the oil, baking powder and flour and combine.
  4. Put the bowl into the freezer for 15 minutes.
  5. Into a plate spill the sugar powder.
  6. Make small balls.
  7. Roll them in the sugar powder.
  8. Put the balls into a baking tray.
  9. Bake the cookies for 10 minutes in 180°C.
  10. Let them chills.
