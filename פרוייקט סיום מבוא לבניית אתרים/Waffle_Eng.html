Original Belgian Waffle

            Authentic and delicious Belgian waffle!

Cool Fact

The word “waffle” has Dutch origins.Although we have proof that waffles may have gotten their start in France or even ancient China, the word “waffle” is descended from the Dutch word for “wafer”. The first use of the actual word “waffle” in the English language was in 1725. We have been a nation of waffle lovers for almost 300 years, people.

Prerequisites

  • 💡Experience Level: Beginners
  • ⏲Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • ⏲Cook Time: 10-12 minutes
  • ⏲Total Time: 20-22 minutes
  • 👥Serving Size: 4

Ingredients

Waffle Base

  • 300 grams flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (top and bottom heat).
  2. Mix all waffle base ingredients in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Place the mixture in a lined baking tray and spread into a square shape.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the waffle reaches a dark golden color.
